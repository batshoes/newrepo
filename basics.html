HTML TAGS

There are a number of tags used in the HyperText Markup Language. Most of them have meaning and a re specifically designed to have influence over the text that they contain.

Heading Tags

h1 is used for main headings.
There are other headings all the way down to h6, the smallest of headings and are "wrapped" around text as such "< h3 > this is a sub heading < /h3 >"
